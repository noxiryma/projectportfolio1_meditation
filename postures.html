Introduction to Postures

The iconic crossed legged sitting position of meditation carries behind it great meaning but the origin of the meditation position is something very simple; this is how the practitioners were sat when they practiced meditation. Even thought this makes your meditation position sound unimportant an uncomfortable position versus a comfortable one makes all the difference on how your practice goes.

Over time and with meditation's growing popularity various postures have been developed to aid the practitioner who doesn't find sitting in a cross legged position comfortable. There are also a number of props that can be used to support the body for holding postures for a longer period of time with minimal effort.
