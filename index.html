The Roots of Meditation

Meditation comes from the Latin term "to ponder" and is used as an umbrella term for a variety of practices.

Some of which are demonstrated on this website on the techniques page.

The origin of meditation has been debated for years and has been argued to have started at the beginnings of our beings with fire gazing meditation to be one of the first practiced 200,000 years ago.

The practices were passed on orally before being commited to writing. The earliest written records appear in the Hindu Vedas around 1500 BCE but they were believed to have been practiced since 3000 BCE.

From Meditation came mindfulness meditation.

Benefits of Meditation:

  • Promotes emotional health by reducing stress, anxiety and depression
  • Accessible anywhere
  • Decreases blood pressure
  • Improves sleep
  • Can generate kindness to yourself and others
  • Helps control addictions and pain
