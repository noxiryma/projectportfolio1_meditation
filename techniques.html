Getting Started

Relax... Meditation comes in many forms and reeps benefits to the practitioner. To start, find a quiet place with little distraction for you to dedicate your time for your meditation.

It is normal for the mind to wander at the beginning of your journey. Be patient with yourself and think of it as a marathon and not a sprint. You wouldn't run a marathon without training first so think of your meditation as training for the longer practices in the future.

See our postures page for recommendations on how to make yourself comfortable for your practice.

Below are some options to help you choose which one is best for you and your practice. You can also vary your pratice by changing your technique.

Mantra Meditation

What is it?

To repeat a phrase or sound in the mind when meditating

Example:

Om on the exhale make the sound Aaaaaaa-uuuuuuu-mmmmmmmm

Yoga Nidra

What is it?

The meaning of yoga nidra is deep rest. Head to your local yoga studio to experience a class.

Noting

What is it?

To mentally note any thoughts, sensations or sounds as the happen during your meditation.

Body scan

What is it?

To mentally scan your body during a meditation with visualisation or just checking to see how each part of the body is feeling in that moment.

Counting

What is it?

Count the breaths on the inhale and exhale. Inhale 1, exhale 2 up to 10 and then repeat.

Count freely up to 100 in a language of your choice. Once you reach 100 repeat. Can also be done in reverse.

Samatha

What is it?

The focus meditation. Focus on a certain point, object or thought during your meditation.

Examples can be a candle.
